"Blindman's Swag"

The blindfolded race is a fun and exciting activity that involves one person wearing a blindfold while the other gives them instructions to complete a task or a race

ABOUT THE EVENT

A couple, where one has to wear a blindfold while the other, gives instruction to complete the at hand task and finish the race.

DATE

23rd and 24th March, 2023

TEAM SIZE

3 couples will play at once in a slot.

RULES

  • Partner 1 will be blindfolded at the start line and Partner 2 will be standing at the finish line.
  • The partners will have to decide their code words for directions, so that it doesn't clash with others and only the couple will be able to recognize the words.
  • Partner 2 will have to give instruction in the code words and lead Partner 1 to the finish line.
  • The race will have obstacles or tasks in between.

REGISTRATION

  • From 5th march onwards.
  • After registration a confirmation email will be sent to you, which will be further required during the day of the event as your ticket.
