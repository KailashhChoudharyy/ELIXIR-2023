About Elixir

RAIT-ACM in collaboration with other committees conducts technical-fest. Wherein RAIT-ACM hosts ELIXIR..

ELIXIR is a popular tech-fest that showcases cutting-edge technology and innovative ideas. It is a space to bring students together With an aim of providing a platform for the Indian student community to develop and showcase their technical prowess. The event features a wide range of activities including games, seminars, workshops, and competitions, all focused on exploring the latest technological advancements.

Witness the grandeur of Elixir'23, it's the year for new experiences!

RAIT ACM STUDENT CHAPTER

RAIT ACM is a Student Chapter of the Elite International level ACM organization. It is a committee focused on the overall development and progress of students in RAIT. We work in different fields like Technical, Creative, Publicity, Video Editing, Management, Social Media, and Editorial.

    Perks Include:
  • One year access to the ACM Digital Library
  • Opportunity to attend seminars, webinars and lectures in a variety of fields, delivered by some distinguished speakers.
  • Get chance to participate as well as organize different technical as well as non-technical events.
  • Enhance impact of your CV
  • Opportunity to represent RAIT ACM at regional as well as national level competitions.
